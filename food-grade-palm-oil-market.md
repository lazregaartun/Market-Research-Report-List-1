<p><p>The food-grade palm oil market primarily consists of two types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. Crude Palm Oil is the unrefined oil extracted directly from fresh fruit bunches and has a higher free fatty acid content, making it suitable for further refining. Palm Olein, refined from CPO, is the liquid fraction and is widely used in cooking and food processing due to its stability and health benefits. Both types play crucial roles in the global food industry, catering to diverse culinary applications.</p></p>
