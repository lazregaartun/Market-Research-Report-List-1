<p><p>Nano Crystal Cellulose (NCC) is available in three primary forms: solid, liquid, and gel. Solid NCC is utilized in various applications, including composites and pharmaceuticals, offering strength and lightweight properties. Liquid NCC serves as a dispersant and stabilizer in personal care and food products, enhancing texture and viscosity. Gel NCC, known for its thickening and emulsifying properties, is commonly used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals, providing improved stability and performance in formulations. Each type plays a distinct role in multiple industries.</p></p>
