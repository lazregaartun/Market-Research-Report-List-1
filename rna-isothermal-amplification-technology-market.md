<p><p>RNA isothermal amplification technology encompasses various market types, primarily including endpoint methods and real-time fluorescence detection. The endpoint method amplifies RNA at a constant temperature, allowing for the analysis of results after the amplification process, making it suitable for simpler diagnostic applications. In contrast, real-time fluorescence detection provides continuous monitoring of the amplification process, enabling quantitative analysis and immediate results. Both methods cater to diverse needs in research and diagnostics, enhancing the efficiency of RNA detection and quantification.</p></p>
