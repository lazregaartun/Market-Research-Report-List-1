<p><p>Fabry-Perot (FP) Laser Diodes are semiconductor devices that utilize the Fabry-Perot interferometer principle to produce coherent light. These laser diodes are characterized by their simple design, compact size, and efficient performance. The FP laser diode operates by having a simple cavity structure, which allows for high-speed modulation and stable output, making them suitable for a range of industrial and consumer electronics.</p><p>The Fabry-Perot Laser Diode (LD) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period.
