<p><p>The ESD vinyl flooring market is primarily divided into two types: tile form and sheet form. Tile form consists of individual tiles that can be easily replaced and installed, making it ideal for areas requiring frequent maintenance. In contrast, sheet form is delivered in larger rolls, providing seamless installation and enhanced coverage, which minimizes seams and potential electrostatic discharge points. Both types are designed to dissipate static electricity effectively, catering to environments like electronics manufacturing and data centers.</p></p>
