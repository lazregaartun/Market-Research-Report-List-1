<p><p>The Oven-Controlled Crystal Oscillator (OCXO) market is characterized by three primary output types: PECL, CMOS, and Sinewave. PECL (Positive Emitter Coupled Logic) outputs are favored for high-speed applications, providing low jitter and maintaining signal integrity. CMOS outputs are popular in digital circuits due to their low power consumption and compatibility. Sinewave outputs are typically used in analog applications, delivering stable frequency signals with low distortion. Each output type caters to specific application needs in communication and precision timing.</p></p>
