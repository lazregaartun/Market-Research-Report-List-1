<p><strong>The Commercial Vacuum Cooker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion</li><li>Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vacuum cooker market encompasses two primary types: immersion and water bath cookers. Immersion vacuum cookers use a direct heating method by immersing food in water or another heat transfer medium, ensuring even cooking and precise temperature control. In contrast, water bath cookers utilize a more consistent temperature by surrounding food containers with heated water in a controlled environment, providing gentle cooking. Both types enhance flavor retention and texture, making them popular in restaurants and food service operations.</p></p>
