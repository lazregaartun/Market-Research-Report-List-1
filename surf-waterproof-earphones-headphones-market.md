<p><p>The Surf Waterproof Earphones and Headphones market is divided into three main types: In-Ear, On-Ear, and Over-Ear. In-Ear models fit snugly within the ear canal, providing passive noise isolation and portability, making them ideal for water sports. On-Ear headphones rest on the outer ear and offer a balance between sound quality and comfort, suitable for casual water activities. Over-Ear headphones encase the entire ear, delivering superior sound quality and comfort, perfect for prolonged use in various water environments.</p></p>
