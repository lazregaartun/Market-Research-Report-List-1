<p><p>The upper arm blood pressure monitor market comprises two primary types: mercury blood pressure monitors and digital blood pressure monitors. Mercury monitors utilize a column of mercury to measure blood pressure accurately, often preferred in clinical settings for their precision. However, due to safety and environmental concerns, their usage is declining. Digital monitors, on the other hand, employ electronic sensors to provide readings, offering convenience and ease of use for home monitoring. Digital devices are increasingly popular for their portability and user-friendly features.</p></p>
