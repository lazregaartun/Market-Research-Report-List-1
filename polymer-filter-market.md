<p><p>The Polymer Filter Market encompasses various filtration types, primarily including Disc Filtration Elements and Candle Filtration Elements. Disc Filtration Elements utilize flat, disc-shaped membranes to separate solids from liquids, offering efficient filtration in diverse applications. In contrast, Candle Filtration Elements are tubular and cylindrical, providing a high surface area for liquid filtration, making them suitable for higher viscosity fluids. Both types serve critical roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemical processing, ensuring the purity and quality of end products.</p></p>
