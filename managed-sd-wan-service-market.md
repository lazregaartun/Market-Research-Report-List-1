<p><p>Managed SD-WAN service market can be divided into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based SD-WAN solutions leverage the scalability and flexibility of cloud infrastructure, enabling organizations to manage their wide-area networks remotely, with minimal hardware requirements. In contrast, On-Premises SD-WAN involves deploying hardware and software within the organization's facilities, offering greater control, security, and customization. Each type caters to different needs, with cloud solutions appealing to those seeking ease of management and on-premises options serving organizations with stringent data governance requirements.</p></p>
