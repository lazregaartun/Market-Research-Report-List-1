<p><strong>The Micro Perforated Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Perforation</li><li>Laser Perforation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro perforated films market comprises two primary types: mechanical perforation and laser perforation. Mechanical perforation involves using physical means, such as needles or punches, to create tiny holes in the film, allowing for controlled breathability and moisture regulation in packaging. In contrast, laser perforation employs high-precision lasers to achieve clean and accurate micro-holes, enhancing the aesthetic appeal and functionality of the film. Both methods cater to various applications in food, medical, and industrial packaging.</p></p>
