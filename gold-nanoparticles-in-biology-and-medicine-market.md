<p><p>Gold nanoparticles have diverse applications in biology and medicine, categorized by their solubility. Water-soluble gold nanoparticles are utilized for drug delivery, imaging, and diagnostics due to their biocompatibility. Oil-soluble variants are suitable for specific applications in lipid environments, such as drug formulation in oil-based systems.
