<p><p>The packaged drinking water market primarily consists of two types: natural mineral water and man-made mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from protected underground aquifers, containing essential minerals and trace elements, known for its inherent health benefits. In contrast, man-made mineral water is created by adding minerals to purified water, designed to mimic the taste and benefits of natural sources. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers, yet differ in sourcing, processing, and mineral composition, influencing market preference and demand.</p></p>
