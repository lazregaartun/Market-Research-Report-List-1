<p><p>The underwear disinfection dryers market includes three main types: UV disinfection, ozone treatment, and low-temperature disinfection. UV disinfection utilizes ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and pathogens effectively, ensuring hygienic underwear. Ozone disinfection employs ozone gas, which oxidizes and kills microorganisms, providing a chemical-free sanitation method. Low-temperature disinfection uses moderate heat combined with moisture to sanitize delicate fabrics without damaging them. Together, these technologies cater to growing hygiene awareness and demand for safe, effective clothing disinfection solutions.</p></p>
