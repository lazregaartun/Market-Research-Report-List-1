<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D scanning system market is broadly categorized into contact and non-contact types. Contact type scanners utilize physical probes to touch and measure objects, providing high precision, making them suitable for detailed engineering applications. Non-contact type scanners, on the other hand, employ technologies like laser or structured light to capture surfaces without direct interaction, offering speed and efficiency, ideal for delicate or large-scale objects. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and cultural heritage conservation.</p></p>
