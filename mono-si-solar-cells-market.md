<p><p>The mono-Si solar cells market is primarily categorized into three types: P-type silicon, N-type silicon, and other types. P-type silicon, doped with boron, is the most common and offers good performance and cost-effectiveness. N-type silicon, doped with phosphorus, provides better efficiency and lower susceptibility to light-induced degradation. Other market types may include advanced technologies like bifacial solar cells or different doping methods. Each type has its advantages, catering to diverse consumer needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
