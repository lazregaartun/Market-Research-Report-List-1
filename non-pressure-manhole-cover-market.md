<p><p>The non-pressure manhole cover market includes two primary types: welding type and thread type. Welding-type manhole covers are permanently attached to the frame, providing a strong seal against water infiltration and ensuring structural integrity. In contrast, thread-type manhole covers utilize threaded connections for easy removal and maintenance, allowing for quick access to underground utilities. Both types are crucial for urban infrastructure, offering durability and safety while catering to different installation and maintenance preferences in various environments.</p></p>
