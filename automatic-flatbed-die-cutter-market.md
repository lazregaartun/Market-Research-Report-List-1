<p><p>The Automatic Flatbed Die-Cutter market features two primary cutting methods: blade cutting and laser cutting. Blade cutting utilizes sharp, hardened blades to precisely cut various materials, offering a cost-effective solution for high-volume production. In contrast, laser cutting employs focused laser beams to achieve intricate and detailed shapes with minimal material wastage. While blade cutting is ideal for thicker materials and larger runs, laser cutting excels in flexibility, allowing for rapid adjustments and complex designs, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
