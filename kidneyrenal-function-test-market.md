<p><p>The Kidney/Renal Function Test market is primarily divided into two types: urine tests and blood tests. Urine tests assess the presence of proteins, electrolytes, and waste products, providing insights into kidney health through samples collected over a specific period. Blood tests measure key markers such as creatinine and blood urea nitrogen (BUN), reflecting the kidneys' ability to filter waste. Both testing types are crucial for diagnosing kidney diseases, monitoring health, and assessing treatment efficacy, catering to healthcare providers and patients alike.</p></p>
