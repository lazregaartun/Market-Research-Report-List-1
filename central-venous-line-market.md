<p><p>The central venous line market is categorized into single-lumen, double-lumen, and triple-lumen lines. Single-lumen catheters are designed for straightforward access, typically used for medication administration, nutrition, or fluid management. Double-lumen catheters allow simultaneous infusion of different solutions, enhancing treatment flexibility. Triple-lumen catheters offer three separate channels, enabling multiple therapies to be administered simultaneously, ideal for complex cases requiring various medications. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, influencing selection based on patient requirements and treatment protocols.</p></p>
