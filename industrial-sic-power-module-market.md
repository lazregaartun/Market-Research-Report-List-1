<p><p>The Industrial SiC Power Module market is primarily divided into two types: Hybrid SiC Modules and Full SiC Modules. Hybrid SiC Modules combine silicon and silicon carbide technologies, providing cost-effective solutions with improved efficiency and thermal performance for applications with moderate power requirements. Both types support advancements in renewable energy, electric vehicles, and industrial automation through enhanced performance.</p></p>
