<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer Oven market encompasses two primary types of titration: coulometric and volumetric. Coulometric titration measures water content by generating iodine electrochemically, making it ideal for low moisture samples. In contrast, volumetric titration involves adding a measured volume of iodine solution to the sample, suitable for higher moisture content applications. Both methods are essential for accurate moisture analysis in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, ensuring product quality and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
