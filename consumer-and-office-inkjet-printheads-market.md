<p><strong>The Consumer and Office Inkjet Printheads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer and office inkjet printheads market is primarily characterized by two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric printheads utilize electric voltage to create pressure that forces ink out, offering precise control and compatibility with a variety of inks. In contrast, thermal printheads use heat to vaporize ink, creating bubbles that push the ink out. Thermal types are generally simpler and lower in cost, while piezoelectric types provide higher quality and faster printing speeds, catering to diverse consumer and office needs.</p></p>
