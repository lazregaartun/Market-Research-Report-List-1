<p><p>Automotive Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) systems enhance engine efficiency and performance by injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber. Turbulent Jet Injection (TJI) systems create turbulent airflow to mix fuel and air more effectively, improving combustion. Piezoelectric fuel injectors offer rapid and precise fuel delivery, enhancing responsiveness and fuel atomization. Water injection systems introduce water vapor during combustion, lowering temperatures and preventing knocking, thereby increasing power and efficiency. Together, these technologies represent advanced solutions for modern engines.</p></p>
