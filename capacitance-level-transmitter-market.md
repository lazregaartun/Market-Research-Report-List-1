<p><p>The capacitance level transmitter market features three primary types: straight rod type, flange type, and screw-type transmitters. Straight rod type transmitters are ideal for applications requiring precise level measurement in narrow spaces. Flange type transmitters are mounted on tanks or vessels, offering robust installation options for various industrial settings. Screw-type transmitters are designed for easy integration into pipelines, ensuring reliable measurement in confined areas. Each type is tailored to specific application needs, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in level sensing.</p></p>
