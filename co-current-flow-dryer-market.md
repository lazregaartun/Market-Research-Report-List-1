<p><p>The co-current flow dryer market consists of vertical and horizontal dryer types, each designed to optimize drying processes in various applications. Vertical dryers operate by allowing both the air and materials to flow in the same direction, enhancing moisture removal efficiency. In contrast, horizontal dryers facilitate material movement across the air flow, promoting even drying and suitable for bulk materials. Both types are vital in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and textiles, catering to diverse drying requirements and space constraints.</p></p>
