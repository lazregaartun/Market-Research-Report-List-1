<p><p>The OCA (Optically Clear Adhesive) market is segmented into two main types: substrate-free optical adhesive and substrate optical adhesive. Substrate-free optical adhesive films are used where no backing material is required, allowing for flexibility in applications such as mobile devices and displays. Substrate optical adhesives, on the other hand, involve a backing layer, providing additional support and stability, which is essential for applications in glass bonding and robust optical assemblies. Both types enhance optical performance and durability.</p></p>
