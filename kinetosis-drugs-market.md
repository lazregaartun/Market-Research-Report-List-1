<p><ul><li>Anticholinergic</li><li>Antihistamines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kinetosis drugs market is categorized into three main types: anticholinergics, antihistamines, and others. Anticholinergic drugs, such as scopolamine, function by blocking signals in the vestibular system to reduce motion sickness symptoms. Antihistamines, like dimenhydrinate and meclizine, alleviate nausea and dizziness by antagonizing histamine receptors. "Others" encompass alternative treatments, including herbal remedies and non-pharmacological approaches. Each type serves to mitigate the effects of kinetosis, catering to varying preferences and severities of motion sickness.</p></p>
