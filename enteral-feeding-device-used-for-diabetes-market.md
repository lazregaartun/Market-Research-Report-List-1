<p><p>The enteral feeding device market for diabetes includes three main types: enteral feeding pumps, enteral feeding tubes, and consumables. </p><p>Enteral feeding pumps are designed to deliver precise nutrition directly into the gastrointestinal tract, often used for patients requiring regulated calorie intake. </p><p>Enteral feeding tubes facilitate the transfer of nutritional formulas into the patient's stomach or intestines. </p><p>Consumables encompass various products like syringes, bags, and connectors that are essential for the effective use of both pumps and tubes, ensuring safe, efficient delivery of nutrition.</p></p>
