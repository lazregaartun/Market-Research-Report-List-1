<p><p>The commercial vehicle air suspension market is segmented into two main types: electronically controlled air suspension and non-electronically controlled air suspension. Electronically controlled air suspension uses advanced technology to adjust the suspension characteristics automatically based on load and road conditions, enhancing ride comfort and vehicle stability. In contrast, non-electronically controlled air suspension relies on traditional pneumatic systems, providing basic load-leveling capabilities without real-time adjustments. Both types serve to improve vehicle performance, safety, and load management in commercial applications.</p></p>
