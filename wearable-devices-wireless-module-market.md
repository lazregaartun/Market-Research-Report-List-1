<p><p>The wearable devices wireless module market comprises two main types: communication modules and positioning modules. Communication modules facilitate data transmission between wearable devices and external networks, enabling functions like notifications, calls, and internet connectivity. Positioning modules, on the other hand, focus on tracking the device's location, utilizing technologies such as GPS or Bluetooth. Together, these modules enhance the functionality and usability of wearables, catering to diverse applications in health monitoring, fitness tracking, and personal safety.</p></p>
