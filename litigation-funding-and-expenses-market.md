<p><p>Litigation funding and expenses encompass various arrangements that support legal claims financially. Conditional Fee Agreements (CFAs) allow lawyers to receive a fee only if the case is won, while Damages-Based Agreements (DBAs) enable solicitors to earn a percentage of the awarded damages. After the Event (ATE) Insurance covers legal costs in case of losing a lawsuit. Third Party Funding involves external investors financing legal actions for a share of the recovery. Additional options include hybrid agreements and contingency fees tailored to specific cases.</p></p>
