<p><p>The Transportation Vehicle Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System market comprises three main types. The Infrared Type utilizes infrared sensors to detect passenger movement by measuring body heat, ensuring accurate counting. The Time-of-Flight Type employs laser or light waves to determine the distance and position of passengers, providing precise data even in crowded conditions. The Stereoscopic Vision Type utilizes dual cameras to create 3D images, allowing for accurate detection of passenger numbers and movement patterns, enhancing operational efficiency for transit authorities.</p></p>
