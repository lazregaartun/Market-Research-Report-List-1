<p><p>The DTS (Distributed Temperature Sensing) market comprises primarily two types: single-mode and multi-mode systems. Single-mode DTS utilizes a single optical fiber to measure temperature with high precision over long distances, making it ideal for applications like oil and gas monitoring. In contrast, multi-mode DTS employs multiple fibers, providing a broader temperature measurement range but with slightly reduced accuracy over shorter distances, suitable for environmental monitoring and industrial applications. Both technologies enhance capabilities in temperature monitoring across various sectors.</p></p>
