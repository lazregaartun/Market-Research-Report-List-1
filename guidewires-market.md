<p><p>The guidewires market is categorized into various types, primarily featuring straight and J-shaped guidewires. Straight guidewires are designed for straightforward navigation through blood vessels, providing flexibility and control during procedures. In contrast, J-shaped guidewires have a curved tip, which aids in advancing through more challenging anatomical structures, enhancing maneuverability. Both types are essential in various medical applications, particularly in interventional cardiology and vascular procedures, ensuring safe and effective access for catheter insertion and other therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
