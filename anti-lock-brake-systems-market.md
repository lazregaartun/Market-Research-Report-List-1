<p><strong>The Anti-lock Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-lock Brake Systems (ABS) market can be categorized into four types based on the number of channels: </p><p>1. **One-channel ABS** primarily suits motorcycles, providing brake control to a single wheel.</p><p>2. **Two-channel ABS** caters to vehicles with front-wheel braking, enabling independent control of the front and rear wheels.</p><p>3. **Three-channel ABS** enhances control by managing two front wheels and one rear wheel, often used in SUVs.</p><p>4. **Four-channel ABS** allows each wheel to be regulated individually, offering superior stability and performance, commonly found in modern passenger vehicles.</p></p>
