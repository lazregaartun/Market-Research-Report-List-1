<p><p>The Marine Electric Vehicles market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)** rely entirely on electric batteries for propulsion, offering zero emissions and high efficiency, ideal for short-distance trips. </p><p>2. **Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs)** combine a conventional engine with an electric motor, allowing for extended range and flexibility in fuel use. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs)** incorporate both electric and diesel engines, optimizing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions without needing external charging. Each type caters to different operational needs within marine environments.</p></p>
